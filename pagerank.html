Sports Pagerank

This utility runs a "pagerank" algorithm on sports teams. Input all the game scores and see where your team ranks.

Since pagerank has to do with pages linking to other pages, we view teams scoring against other teams as the losing team having a link to the winning team. If this makes no sense, google "Google pagerank algorithms". There are good explanations out there.
